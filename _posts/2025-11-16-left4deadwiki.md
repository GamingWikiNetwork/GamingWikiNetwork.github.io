The **Left 4 Dead Wiki** is the latest addition into the Gaming Wiki Network!

**Left 4 Dead Wiki**, originally created by ShadoW on Wikia on May 11, 2008, and migrated to Miraheze on August 7, 2025, aims to be the most complete and accurate source of information about the Left 4 Dead series that anyone can edit.

Check it out at [https://left4deadwiki.com/wiki/Left_4_Dead_Wiki](https://gamingwikinetwork.org/warn/left4deadwiki)!
